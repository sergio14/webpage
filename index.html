About Myself

I’m an enthusiast and creative scientist with wide experience in the study of complex data. I enjoy exploring crude data collected from sensors, social networks, satellites or biological sources, and employing data analysis tools to mine hidden gems or predicting underlying behaviors.

My professional background include data mining, machine learning, computing, visualization, database management, agronomy, molecular biology, genetics, microbiology and immunology.

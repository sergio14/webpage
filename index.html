About me

I’m an enthusiast and creative scientist with deep experience in the study of complex data. I enjoy exploring messy data collected from sensors, social networks, satellites or biological sources and employ data mining and computational tools to mine hidden treasures or predict underlying behaviors.

My professional background includes data mining, machine learning, computation, information visualization, data base management, molecular biology, genetics, microbiology and immunology.
